Questions

FAQ

Why one wallet instead of per-product pricing?

Because you are buying access to one infrastructure, not a bundle of APIs. A single balance of Orves Credits removes the friction of deciding budgets per product before you know your mix.

Why does Verifiable Knowledge not charge per token?

Charging tokens would put it in the same category as the models. It sells persistent infrastructure, so it meters knowledge operations — ingest, update, retrieve, consolidate — not words generated.

Do external model costs come through Orves?

The design separates Orves usage from third-party LLM costs — pass-through or bring-your-own-key where it makes sense. Final mechanics published with rates.

Why are there no dollar prices on this page?

Because we have not finished measuring unit costs, and we do not publish numbers we cannot defend. The same discipline as our benchmarks.

Can I start small?

Yes — 15,000 free credits, one product, one problem. Grow when it earns it.
