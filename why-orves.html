Why Orves

A category, not another tool.

Verifiable Knowledge Infrastructure: the layer under AI systems that makes knowledge provable — where it came from, how it changed, whether it can be trusted.

The thesis

Every major computing wave created new infrastructure.

Storage. Networking. Payments. Compute. AI created another: knowledge. Reasoning became cheap — knowledge became the bottleneck.

We don’t build AI. We build knowledge AI can trust.

Context ≠ Knowledge
Retrieval ≠ Memory
Storage ≠ Understanding
Answers ≠ Evidence
The gap

Everyone sells answers. Nobody governs knowledge.

RAG, agents, AI search, knowledge bases — the market optimizes the last step and improvises everything under it. The result is fluent systems standing on unverifiable foundations.

The discipline

Nothing is advanced by narrative.

The engineering culture behind the platform, visible in the product.

Every vendor wins their own benchmark

Which is why ours is designed to be blind: pre-registered metrics, versioned datasets, reported n. The point is to find weaknesses, not to advertise.
