Why it is different

Because of what it stands on.

Other systems store what you tell them; Brain can prove what it knows. Parser understands your sources losslessly. Canonical makes the knowledge durable, versioned and traceable. The Observatory keeps it synchronized with reality. When evidence is insufficient — Brain abstains instead of inventing.
