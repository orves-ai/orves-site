Method

Designed so we cannot flatter ourselves.

Every vendor wins their own benchmark. Ours is built to find our weaknesses first.

Pre-registered

Metrics, thresholds and datasets are fixed before the run. No metric shopping after the fact.

Gold is corrigible

Gold sets are scientific artifacts: versioned, adjudicated, corrected through a recorded process — never silently edited.

Abstention is scored

UNKNOWN is a measured outcome. A system that hides its abstentions is optimizing the wrong thing.

Champions re-elected

Every capability runs a permanent championship. Champions keep their place only while they win — including ours.

Scoped numbers

No naked scores: every published number carries its n, its dataset version and its confusion matrix.

Detection proven

Evaluation layers are validated by deliberate fault injection — we prove the benchmark can catch failure before trusting its green.
